About Us

The Ashabaab Quranic Community is a dedicated Islamic academy focused on nurturing the next generation through Quranic knowledge, character building, and understanding of Islam. We believe youth are the future of our community.

Our Courses

  • Qaida: Foundation for proper Quranic recitation
  • Hifz: Memorization of the Holy Quran with supervision
  • Tajweed: Correct pronunciation and rules of recitation
  • Duas & Ahadees: Essential prayers and sayings of the Prophet (PBUH)
  • Explain Islam: Understanding Islam in a simple, impactful way
